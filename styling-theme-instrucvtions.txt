##High-level look & feel##

Overall vibe: clean, corporate, “white-glove” marketing site with generous whitespace, big headlines, and friendly iconography. Sections breathe; copy is concise; CTAs are bold and high-contrast. 
boldrimpact.com

Layout rhythm: stacked sections with strong hero, benefit tiles, testimonial blocks, capability lists, and repeated CTA. Content width remains centered and readable. 
boldrimpact.com

##Typography##
Font family: modern humanist/geometric sans. Use Inter, system-ui, -apple-system, Segoe UI, Roboto, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;

Type scale (desktop):

H1: 48–56px / 1.1 line-height; heavy/semibold (hero)

H2: 36–40px / 1.2 (section leads)

H3: 24–28px / 1.25 (tile headings)

Body: 18–20px / 1.6 (readable marketing copy)

Small: 14–16px / 1.5 (eyebrow, captions)

Color use: headlines near-black; body gray-600; links primary orange with underline on hover. This mirrors the high-contrast heading + muted body pattern visible on the ecommerce page. 
boldrimpact.com


##Spacing & layout grid##

Container max-width: 1200–1280px (content), center with padding-inline: 24px mobile / 40px desktop.

Section padding:

Desktop: 96px top & bottom (hero up to 128px)

Tablet: 72px

Mobile: 48px

Vertical rhythm: use an 8-pt system (4/8/12/16/24/32/48/64/96…). Headline→subhead: 16–20px; subhead→body: 16px; body→CTA: 24px.

Columns: 12-col grid; common content blocks use 6/12 and 4/12 patterns. Benefit tiles often display in 3× on desktop, 2× tablet, 1× mobile (as seen in “Why choose…” section). 
boldrimpact.com

##Cards / tiles / testimonials##

Cards: white surface, subtle shadow 0 1px 2px rgba(0,0,0,.06), 0 8px 24px rgba(0,0,0,.06), border-radius: 12px, padding: 24–32px, icon/image top, short H3, body text, optional CTA/link. This mirrors their benefit tiles & testimonial blocks. 
boldrimpact.com

Dividers: use 1px var(--color-border) or section background alternation --color-surface-alt.


##Hero section##

Structure: eyebrow (optional), big H1, supportive H2/subhead, short paragraph, primary CTA, secondary CTA (ghost), hero image/illustration to the right or below on mobile. This follows their hero pattern (“We delight your customers…”, subhead, CTA). 
boldrimpact.com

Hero spacing: 128px top / 96px bottom desktop; stack on mobile with 48px gaps.


##Iconography & imagery##

Icons: simple line/duotone with brand orange accents; size 40–56px in tiles.

Image treatment: clean photography/illustrations with ample whitespace; rounded corners 12–16px.


##Lists & capability grids##

Bulleted lists: comfortable line-height: 1.6 and margin-block: 12px; checkmark or simple dot; keep left alignment and consistent icon color (primary). Reflects their capability breakdowns and “Leading eCommerce companies trust…” lists. 
boldrimpact.com

##Footer##

Background: white (or very light gray), multi-column link lists, certification badges, and social links; generous padding 48–64px. This structure shows on both the ecommerce and home pages.

##Accessibility & usability notes##

Keep contrast ≥ WCAG AA (the brand orange on white is fine for buttons; avoid using orange for long body text).

Ensure focus rings on all interactive elements (use the orange ring token above).

Maintain 44×44px touch targets on mobile CTAs.
